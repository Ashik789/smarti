Residential Solutions

You can benefit from a complete, flexible system that takes control of lighting, audio, room climate, security and more. Remotely control and personalize your smart home right from your smartphone or tablet anywhere, anytime.

Lighting control

Automated lighting can transform any rooms and lifestyles with full color and tunable white lighting effect. With either our specially developed LED lights or any third-party lights, they can be controlled to fit your needs.

Multi-room audio

Beyond a music system for home, audio can do so much more along with other integrated systems. Wake up to upbeat music, choose a sound to replace your doorbell, receive text-to-speech

Access control

Get notified when a visitor rings at your door and then choose whether you want to give them door access or interact with them through the intercom via the app.

Read More
Security

The home stays on guard when you go to sleep at night. In any case of an intruder, the building will know to activate a four-phase smart alarm system, starting with a smartphone notification. Your home puts up a strong defense against intruders.

Energy management

Every function works together with energy efficiency in mind. You save costs without any extra effort because your smart home figures it out for you.

Room climate

Keep all your rooms at a comfortable temperature by individually controlling each room with intelligent interaction between shading, solar panels, sensors and more.

Shading control
