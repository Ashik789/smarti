i-Smarti Technologies

i-Smarti Technologies, LLC., is system integrator service technologies company specializing in low voltage and serve different industries. i-Smarti Technologies, LLC., has been in business since June 2005 along with its parent companies. i-Smarti Technologies, LLC., has a proven track record with different sized smart and security solutions, completing the installation of low voltage wired and wireless systems for 500+ customers annually in middle east and gulf area. We employ professionals all hired for a specific combination of education, skills and experience assuring that each are highly successful in their positions
